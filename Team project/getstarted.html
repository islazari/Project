Uth tutoring

Welcome

Uth tutoring is a website that helps you communicate, work and learn with other individuals more efficiently and productively. It is a platform, made by students for students, that can provide you help with your studies since its purpose is to arrange a meeting between you and a volunteer in order to get tutored by him for free! But what if you don't feel that you need help and instead you feel like helping one of your co-students? Simple. You can sign up in our website and become a tutor yourself. Whether you struggle with your studies or doing just fine, a simple sign up can get you closer with great individuals from your department you might never heard of in order to help each other and make our university community a better place. Our platform is currently active only within the University of Thessaly's boundaries and more specifically in the city of Volos, but we are hoping to expand soon enough and make this website a universal solution for every student in the world.

How To:

1.This is the first thing you see when you enter our website. You will notice a Sing up now button in the middle of your screen. Click it.

2.As you can see the button will immediately redirect you to our register page.

Please fill out all the fields and click the submit button

You will be redirected to Thank you for registering! page which means your registration was successful. Now you need to login to gain full access to our website. Click on the green Log in button as shown in the next picture:

3. You are now redirected to our Login page:

Fill out the form with your information and press the login button as shown above. You can also access this website from our home page if you already have an account just by clicking the green Log in button at the right top corner of your screen.

4.If your credentials were correct your login was successful, and you will be redirected to your home page. You will notice a personal welcome message and also a navigation menu on the top right of your screen.

5. The first option of this menu is the button BOOK A TUTOR

By clicking it you will be redirected to our book page

and you will be able to see all available tutors and their information in detail regarding the names, the department they belong, the courses they can help you with, their availability concerning the days and the hours, and in the last column you will notice a Book now button. Decide which one you prefer and click the Book Now option.

After you decide which tutor is the most suitable for you, you can click the BOOK NOW button. This will take you to their youcanbook.me page where you can setup your meeting. The tutor will contact you in order to confirm and there you have it. You have just set up your first tutoring lesson.

6. The second option of the menu is the button BECOME A TUTOR

If you click it, you will be redirected to a registration form quite similar to the first one.

If you wish to become a tutor, please fill out all the fields and click the submit button as shown in the next picture.

We will receive your application and a confirmation e-mail from us will be sent to you. We will review your credentials and if everything works out, you will be added in the tutors list within the day and you will be able to receive tutoring requests in your youcanbook.me page.

If your tutor registration was successful after clicking the submit button you will be redirected to a Thank you page. Click the green Home button in the middle of your screen and will be redirected to your home page

7.The third option of the menu is the button CONTACT US

By clicking it you will be redirected to our contact form. Fill out your information and the message you want to send us and click the submit button.

We will receive an email with your information, and we will contact you, as soon as possible. If the email is sent correctly you will be transferred to a Thank you web page where by clicking the green Home button you will be redirected to your home page.

You can also contact us through our social media pages by clicking the appropriate icons as shown in the picture bellow.

8.The last option of the menu is the button LOGOUT

By clicking it your session ends and you will be redirected to our home page as shown in the pictures bellow.
